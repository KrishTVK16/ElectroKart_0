Our Story

Founded in 2018, ElectroKart began as a small startup with a big dream: to make cutting-edge technology accessible to every Indian household. What started as a passion project in a small Bangalore office has grown into one of India's most trusted e-commerce platforms for electronics and gadgets.

Over the years, we've partnered with over 200K verified sellers and delivered millions of products across 1000+ cities. Our commitment to authenticity, quality, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of over 100 million registered users.
